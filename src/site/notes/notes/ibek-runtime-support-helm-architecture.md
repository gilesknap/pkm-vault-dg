## Top-level Tree

Under **Services**, three branches are identified:

- **helm shared** — a shared Helm layer consumed by both `ibek-runtime-support` and individual services.
- **ibek-runtime-support** — organises its Helm chart content into subfolders that mirror the pattern established in [[ibek-mgmt\|ibek-mgmt]]. Each subfolder contains instance-specific YAML files such as `XTA.ibek.apps.yaml`.
- **services** — splits into service A (containing `conify`) and service B (containing `config.`).

A `.templates` directory is connected by a large looping arrow annotated **schema**, indicating that templates drive or validate the YAML schema for subfolders.

## Concepts

- **Subfolders pattern** — `ibek-runtime-support` reuses the same subfolder convention as `ibek-mgmt`, reducing cognitive overhead across projects.
- **Schema loop** — `.templates` feeds a schema back into the subfolders, enforcing structure on `XTA.ibek.apps.yaml` and peers.
- **conify** — appears under service A; likely a configuration generation or templating tool within the services layer.
